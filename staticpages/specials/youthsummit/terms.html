1. Contest

 The “Power of Ideas” & “Art Attack” Contest (hereinafter referred to as "Contest") is set to generate innovative and new ideas. In „Power of Idea“ the participants can send us their original opinion in writing  in ENGLISH language not exeeding 20 words on the subject “why your mind rocks” and in “Art Attack” the participant can send us designs/artistic vision for the word/tile “mindrocks” with participation of talented and interested members of general public. A partcipant can participate in both contest. 

The Contest is conducted by “Living Media India Ltd“ (hereinafter referred to as LMIL)  in connection with its India “Today Youth Summit”, for more information about LMIL you my visit the website www.Intoday.com


2. Contest goals

The main goal of the contest is providing an oppertunity to the talented and interested  members of general public  in order to professionally showcase their designs/ artistic vision and a chance to win prizes if the design is selected by our expert judges.


3. Eligibility

The competition targets all yongsters who are Indian Citizens and residing within the  territory of India and  not more than 25 years of age on the date of sending the designs for this contest. Prospective entrants must first register their name in www.indiatoday.in/youthsummit and accept the Terms and Condition of competition before submitting any of their designs. Only a natural person is entitled to take part. The Living Media India Limitred or India Today Group Company employees or their relatives or family members are not elligible to take part in this competition.

4. How to participate

To participate in the Contest the following steps should be taken into account:

Online through the website of LMIL having url  www.indiatoday.in/youthsummit

  • Upload your own computer generated or freehand design(s)
  • Designs may be either created by computer-generated programs or drawn freehand.
  • They must then be converted to JPG, EPS or AI format for uploading to the website with a minimum width of 1024px and maximum width of 1200px
  • The file size must not exceed 1 MB.
  • The image should reach LMIL on or before 15th August 2011 midnight and entry received after the said date shall be rejected
