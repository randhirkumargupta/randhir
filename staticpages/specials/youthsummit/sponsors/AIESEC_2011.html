Youth Partner
AIESEC

AIESEC is an international platform for young people to explore and develop their leadership potential to have a positive impact in society.

Our Nature
AIESEC is a global, non-political, not-for-profit, youth-run, independent organizations & does not discriminate on the basis of race, colors, sex, sexual orientation, creed, religion, national or ethnic origin.

Our Vision
Peace and fulfillment of human kind’s potential.

Our Work
By developing individuals, we contribute to the development of our communities with an overriding commitment to international co-operation and understanding. AIESEC provides its members with an integrated development experience comprised of leadership opportunities, international internships and participation in a global learning environment. Each year our platform provides them with:

. 7700 Leadership opportunities
. Over 470 conferences to our membership of more than 38,000 students
. 10,000 work abroad experiences through our International Internships
. Virtual and Physical Learning Networks

Leadership opportunities
Through the management of over 700 offices at the local, national and international level, we provide about 7700 leadership opportunities to our members each year. This provides our members with valuable and practical experience in both hard and soft skills in management and leadership.

Conferences
We organize approximately 470 conferences each year to train and develop our members. These conferences provide the opportunity to practice leadership, gain important trainings and develop a network.

Exchange
As our main area of business, the AIESEC exchange program provides 10000 graduate the opportunity to work abroad yearly in the fields of management, technical, educational and developmental issues every year.

Learning Networks
Learning Networks aim to connect stakeholders to share and exchange knowledge and skills around common topics of interest and to enable individuals to understand networks and experience in their area of passion.

AIESEC Unique Approach
The key difference between AIESEC members and other students are their entrepreneurial, business skills and leadership experience that they develop. All AIESECers take part in a professional development cycle that aims to equip our people with:

Leadership Experience
AIESEC members have the opportunity to lead teams in different areas; planning, decision making, public speaking, and communicating and turning their ideas into action.

Multifunctional Skills
AIESEC members get practical experience in all facets of running an organization, including sales and marketing, customer service, recruitment, evaluation, budgeting, planning, project management and presentation skills.
