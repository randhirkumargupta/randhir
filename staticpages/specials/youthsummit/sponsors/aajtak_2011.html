TV Partner
Aaj Tak

The TV Today Network (TVTN) launched Aaj Tak as a 24-hour Hindi news channel in December 2000 in line with the Group's mission of exploring the television medium. Aaj Tak emerged as a clear trendsetter by making news a part of the average Indian's daily life. After establishing Aaj Tak as the most successful and preferred 24-hour Hindi news channel in India, TVTN launched it in the US, UK and Dubai. The response from the Indian diaspora has been overwhelming. The channel's focus on accuracy and timeliness of content and its innovative presentation style has been well facilitated by its strong credo of "Sabse Tez" journalism. This has ensured its leadership position and allowed it to set the agenda for news broadcasting in the country. Not surprisingly, Aaj Tak has been awarded the "Best Hindi News Channel" by the Indian Television Academy for nine successive years. Aaj Tak - Sabse Tez.
