  • III. PROCEDURE TO PARTICIPATE IN THE COMPETITION:
  • Level 1 – Registration and online selection test.
  • 1 The quiz is open to school students studying in Class 9-12, in the academic year 2016-17, who would represent their school.
  • 2 The students should be citizens of India and residing and studying in a school within the territorial boundaries of India.
  • 3 Teams need to fill out the online entry form uploaded on http://goo.gl/forms/HYcXX8I6fz, last date of registration is 25th May 2016.
  • 4 Team members also need to email us recent passport size photographs of each member. Email id: newswiz@toknowledge.com
  • 5 Each team needs to apply as a team of two. If the composition of the team changes at any stage subsequent to selection, then they would be disqualified.
  • 6 Each school can nominate upto 3 teams to take part in the online test.
  • 7. Team participation should mean both members represent the same school between Classes 9-12 in the academic year of 2016-2017.
  • 8 Teams must provide written approval from their school principal regarding their being permitted and authorized to represent the school, and to participate in the recordings, if selected.
  • 9 Both team members should be fluent in written as well as spoken English.
  • Level 2:-
  • 1 The shortlisted teams post the online written test will have to face a live video call test with 3 questions to be answered, to enable selection of the final teams that will make it to the TV rounds. Such video calls shall be recorded by the Producer.
  • 2 The team shall be connected via a video conference call or if need be, each member of the team will be connected on the video call separately, with Producer and the team needs to answer 3 questions. The team will have 10 seconds to answer each question. During the live video call test, the teams cannot look out for any help for the answers.
  • 3 At the beginning of the call, the teams shall introduce themselves (names, school, team name etc), shall share as to why they should be selected for the contest. Further, the team should also share their interests, & views on the latest national/ international breaking news they are following. This may be candid and spontaneous, and are meant to test how naturally they are able to articulate and express themselves.
  • 4 Post the introduction, 3 questions shall be asked to the teams with a time period of maximum 10 seconds for each question.
  • 5 The contestants are recommended to have an internet speed of around 8 mbps and the computer configuration to have at least 4 GB RAM to enable a lag-free video call. Team members need to have a Skype account, whose details/username shall be shared with the Producer in advance.
  • 6 A maximum of 3 attempts shall be made to connect the team on the video call. Also in case there are connectivity issues during an on-going video call, maximum 3 attempts to reconnect with the team shall be done.
  • LEVEL 3 – Final Registrant(s)
  • 1 On successful completion of Level 1 and Level 2, all selected Registrants will be evaluated and shortlisted.
  • 2 We are looking out for 27 teams from across the country.
  • 3 Final selection of 27 teams for the TV rounds will be on the basis of merit, personality evaluation on the basis of viva & video calls made, as well as overall geographical & gender representation. No test results will be shared or queries will be entertained in regard to the final teams selected.
  • 4 The decision of the Company/Producers in connection with the Audition including but not limited to short listing the Auditionees will be final & binding and is non-contestable. In case of any dispute or difference in respect of this Audition, the decision of the Company shall be final and binding on all concerned.
  • 5 The competition structure is 9 prelims, 3 semis and 1 final. Winner goes through at each stage.
  • 6 The format of the actual quizzing rounds per episode will be shared with the selected teams for the show post declaration of results, and from time to time, prior to the recording, as may be required.
  • 7 Kindly notify us about your exam and holiday schedules. This is important to plan our schedules without having issues at a later stage.
  • 8 There will be one episode recorded and aired every week starting second week July. In the event a team makes it to the semi finals, the same would be recorded in the month of September. If a team goes on to make it to the Finals of the series the same would be recorded in the month of October.
  • 9 For queries please call on 011 – 29244134 and/or email us at newswiz@toknowledge.com.

There is no one better equipped than Rajdeep to host such a show considering that he has spent 26 years reporting, editing and anchoring news.

SIDDHARTHA BASU

Basu is the undisputed quiz man of India. He is the pioneer who hosted and produced the iconic Quiz Time on Doordarshan and also produced the marquee Kaun Banega Crorepati. He has been the mind behind Mastermind India, University Challenge and India's Child Genius. Now for the first time on news television in India, he is bringing a news-based quiz show.
