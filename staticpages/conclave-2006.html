FLASHBACK 2006: BRIDGING THE DIVIDE
March 10-11, 2006, Taj Palace Hotel, New Delhi
INDIA TODAY Conclave 2006 brought some of the best minds in the business to chalk out the best way to bridge the two Indias. Economists came together to discuss massive budget allocations that were supposed to eradicate poverty. Chief Ministers debated India’s federal fabric and the Centre’s role as the watchful big brother. Opinion makers weighed India’s health scenario in the context of diseases like AIDS. The focus was also on feminism and Indian women. And whether Bollywood had the needed cultural prowess to entertain the entire world – from Argentina to Zaire. INDIA TODAY Conclave 2006 became a host of issues that gave a new direction to help India play her role in the new world order.
