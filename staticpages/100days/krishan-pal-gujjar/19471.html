Krishan Pal Gurjar

MoS for Road Transport, Highways and Shipping

Krishan Pal Gurjar has been made MoS for Social Justice & Empowerment. Earlier, he was the MoS for Road Transport and Highways, Shipping. Gujjar, born 1957, won the Lok Sabha election from Faridabad seat in Haryana. He has been a member of the Haryana assembly. He is a former Haryana BJP chief and a veteran in state politics.
