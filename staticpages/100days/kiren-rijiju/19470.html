Kiren Rijiju

MoS for Home Affairs

Kiren Rijiju is the MoS for Home Affairs. Kiren Rijiju, born 1971, won the Lok Sabha election from Arunachal West seat. He was a member of the 14th Lok Sabha too. He has never held a ministerial post earlier but has been a member of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Energy.
