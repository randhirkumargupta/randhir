Cityspeak

The following were the discernable trends in some of the major cities
 
 

LUCKNOW

Has the highest percentage of men who declared they had sex before (84).
Has the lowest percentage of men thinking that sex is “very important” in their life (40 per cent) although, 49 per cent said sex was “important”.
80 per cent of men expect or had expected their wife to be a virgin, while the Indian average is of 72 per cent. Only three states score higher or similar on that issue: Jaipur (83), Chandigarh (82) and Patna (80).
Has the second lowest percentage of men thinking that premarital sex is perfectly fine (16). Chennai is first with 14 per cent. Most men in Lucknow said it depended on the partners’ commitment (42); this is the highest score in the country for that answer.
Only 9 per cent of men would marry a woman who admitted to having had premarital-sex with one or few partners. This is one of the lowest percentages in Indian states. Only Ahmedabad and Kolkata score lower.
48 per cent of men say they think poorly of women who agree to have sex quickly. The national average is 43.
Has one of the highest percentages of men thinking that men should have sex only with women to whom they are committed (81). Only Jaipur and Patna score higher or similar there (81 and 82 per cent).
68 per cent of men said they think women often take sex too seriously and confuse it with commitment. The national average is 57.
47 per cent of men in Lucknow said that they sometimes find sex in marriage boring. On average, 40 per cent of Indian men feel the same.
Has the second highest percentage of men who are put off by women’s disinterest in sex (52).
Has the lowest percentage of men thinking that a woman’s sex appeal lies in her beauty (24). However, it has also the lowest percentage of men thinking that it lies in her brains (9).
81 per cent of men say they find intelligent women attractive. That is above average (71).
Has the lowest percentage of men saying that they are extremely possessive with women (23) and the highest percentage of men saying that they are not possessive at all (23).
33 per cent of men say they have had extra-marital sex. The national average is 27. The highest percentage is found in Patna (39).
55 per cent of men would rather talk to an unfaithful partner to try to sort things out. It is the second highest percentage after Patna (57). The average is 38.

83 per cent men said they wanted their brides to be virgins.
83 per cent say they would refuse to marry women who admitted to any kind of pre-marital sex.
80 per cent said women are as enthusiastic as men in bed.
53 per cent said they had never masturbated.
15 per cent oral sex is very important for them.
30 per cent said they would be okay if their partners fantasized in bed about other men.
55 per cent men voted for pornography as a sex tool.
15 per cent men had had at least one homosexual experience.
51 per cent talked sex with their women friends.
50 per cent did not associate women’s clothes with their liberation.
35 per cent men said they boasted sometimes about their sexual encounters.
49 per cent said they had sex only after marriage.
49 per cent said they were put off women’s disinterest in sex.
High Frequency: 24 per cent said they had sex daily.
Most insensitve: 11 per cent said that the woman’s mood does not matter, 17 per cent said they insisted on sex very often and 41 per cent said they insisted sometimes.
38 per cent men-more than anywhere else in the country said they tried sex before a mirror.
31 per cent had had sex with a paid sex worker.
96 per cent had had extramarital sex.


CHANDIGARH

48 per cent said pre-marital sex was perfectly fine.
80 per cent said women confused sex with commitment.
26 per cent said the appeal of a woman lay in her sexual skills.
38 per cent say they would love it if their partner masturbated in front of them.
46 per cent found leopard print underclothes sexually appealing.
70 per cent voted for pornography.
Chandigarh put the salwar kameez over the sari as their favourite female attire with 35 per cent voting for it, over and above the national average of 54.
13 per cent say they always boasted about their sexual encounters.
40 per cent said they had shared their homosexual experiences with their wives.
45 per cent said they had multiple orgasms.
58 per cent much higher than the national average of 39 that they wanted more variety in sex.

39 per cent admitted to extra marital sex, higher than the national average of 27 per cent.
31 per cent said they had extra marital sex with wife’s cousins or friends.
49 per cent said they had more than one-a few-sexual partners outside marriage.
67 per cent said their wives complained of headache when they suggested sex.
45 per cent had had sex before marriage.
23 per cent admitted to paid sex.

28 per cent say they sometimes have anal sex with their wives/girlfriends.

CHENNAI, BANGALORE, HYDERABAD

Chennai has the highest percentage of men saying that sex is very important in their life (63 per cent). Bangalore and Hyderabad have average percentages on that issue (53 and 48 per cent).

Around 70 per cent of men in the South expect or had expected their wife to be a virgin (Chennai: 70 per cent, Bangalore: 70 per cent, Hyderabad: 68 per cent). This more than in Delhi or Mumbai, but less than in the northern cities of Lucknow, Jaipur, Patna or Chandigarh.

In Chennai, 28 per cent of men would marry or have married a woman who admits to having had pre-marital sex with one or few partners. This is the highest percentage in the country. However, only 12 would do so in Bangalore and Hyderabad. On average, South India score higher on that issue than North India (Delhi not included).

Only 28 per cent of men in Chennai, 27 in Bangalore and 41 in Hyderabad think that women take sex too seriously and confuse it with commitment. The national average is 57.

Only 74 per cent in Chennai, 65 in Bangalore, and 71 in Hyderabad think that sex with their wife is a marital right. These are the lowest percentages in the country (average: 80).

57 per cent of men in Bangalore think that a woman’s sex appeal lie in her beauty. This is the highest percentage. Chennai (39 per cent) and Hyderabad (36) are also above average (34).

Only 42 per cent of men in Hyderabad think women are as enthusiastic about sex as men. This is the lowest percentage.

46 per cent of men in Hyderabad said they never had any homosexual experience. This is the lowest percentage. On the contrary, Chennai and Bangalore have average answer here (respectively 70 and 69).

If they found out that their partner was unfaithful, very few men in South India would try to talk and sort things out (Chennai: 26 per cent, Bangalore: 22, Hyderabad: 25). The national average is 38, and, Chandigarh (24) excepted, the South has the lowest percentages. 21 per cent of men in Hyderabad would tell their wife that they have the right to do the same. This is the highest percentage for that answer.

28 per cent of in men in Chennai and 25 per cent in Hyderabad would tell their wife if they had a casual one-night stand. These are the lowest percentages with Ahmedabad (25).

Only 64 per cent of men in Chennai, 50 in Bangalore, and 53 in Hyderabad have never asked a woman for a sexual favour in return for a job, an assignment and a promotion. These are by far the three lowest rates in the whole country. The national average is 73.

58 per cent of men in Chennai do not think poorly of women who agree to have sex quickly. This is the highest percentage. Bangalore: 47. Hyderabad: 44. National average: 47.

Only 54 per cent of men in Chennai, 45 in Bangalore and 62 in Hyderabad find intelligent women attractive. These are the three lowest percentages in India. The national average is 71.

26 per cent of men in Chennai said they never use condoms. It is the highest rate. On the contrary, Bangalore has the lowest rate of men who never use condoms (9). The national average is 16. (Hyderabad: 15).

75 of men in Hyderabad had sex for the first time between 18 and 26. This is the highest percentage. National average: 65. Chennai: 60. Bangalore: 55.

35 per cent of men in Chennai said that having repeated sex with the same woman is what put them off the most about sex with a woman. This is the highest percentage for this answer.

32 per cent of men in Hyderabad have sex daily. This is the highest percentage in the country. National average: 20. Chennai: 21. Bangalore: 28.

24 per cent of men in Hyderabad say they always give oral sex to their wife/girlfriend; 26 per cent say they often do so. These are the highest percentage in the country. National average for these two answers: respectively 9 and 12.

15 per cent of men in Chennai and 13 in Bangalore said that they think that whether or not their partner has an orgasm doesn’t matter. These are the highest percentages. National average: 8. Hyderabad: 8.

66 per cent of men in Chennai prefer the men on top position. This is the highest percentage. National average: 47. Bangalore: 32. Hyderabad: 33.

21 per cent of men in Hyderabad, 14 in Bangalore and 13 in Chennai said they had sex with a sex worker or a call girl many times. 24 per cent of men in Hyderabad, 29 in Bangalore and 28 in Chennai said they have done it sometimes. These are by far the highest rates in India. National average: 11 and 19 respectively.

11 per cent of men in Chennai and 16 in Hyderabad said they would include their spouse/girlfriend in multipartner sex. Highest. National average: 7. Bangalore: 8.

21 per cent of men in Hyderabad said they have tried wife/girlfriend swapping. Highest by very far. National average: 7. Second highest: Mumbai, Chennai and Chandigarh with 9. Bangalore: 8.

23 per cent of men in Chennai, 20 in Bangalore and 15 in Hyderabad have had sex with a family member. By far the three highest. National average: 8.

54 per cent of men in Chennai said that the frequency of sex in their marriage has decreased after they had had children. Highest with Chandigarh. National average: 37. Bangalore: 30. Hyderabad: 41 (number 3).

32 per cent of men in Chennai, 21 in Bangalore and 35 in Hyderabad had extra-marital sex. National average: 27. Highest: Patna with 39. 19 per cent of men in Bangalore answered “Don’t know/Can’t say” to this question.
