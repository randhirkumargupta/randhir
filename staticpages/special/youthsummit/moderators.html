Aroon Purie
Chairman & Editor-in-Chief of the India Today Group
Inaugural Session: Dream Big, Do Bigger
The founding Chairman & Editor-in-Chief of the India Today Group, Aroon Purie heads India's most respected and diversified media conglomerate which publishes a host of magazines and a daily newspaper. The Group has four TV News channels, a Radio channel and owns Thomson Press, India's largest commercial printing plant. An alumnus of the London School of Economics and a qualified Chartered Accountant, Purie has served on the Board of many prestigious institutions in India and abroad and is presently Chairman of the Board of the International Federation of the Periodical Press (FIPP). He is the recipient of numerous awards for his outstanding contribution to Journalism in India and was awarded the Padma Bhushan, amongst India's highest civilian honours, in 2001.


Sharda Ugra
Senior Editor, Cricinfo
Luncheon Keynote Session: Keep Your Eye on the Board
Sharda Ugra, now Senior Editor for Cricinfo, the world’s biggest cricket website, has been a sportswriter for the past 20 years, working with Mumbai tabloid Mid-Day and national daily, The Hindu, before putting in ten years at India Today. She was also Indian stringer for Radio Australia, the ABC’s overseas service, and worked on a study commissioned by the British Sports Council on Asian and Afro-Carribean cricket in East London. She has written on cricket for publications in England and Australia, both popular and academic, won Sports Journalists Federation of India (SJFI) awards for sports writing and worked with former New Zealand captain John Wright on Indian Summers, his memoirs of his years coaching India.


Kalli Purie
Chief Operating Officer of India Today Group Digital
Session Technology: The Magic of Sixth Sense
Kalli Purie is Chief Operating Officer of India Today Group Digital. She brings a rich experience of launching and managing a wide range of media properties from a tabloid, to a digital newspaper to mobile contests to an internal marketing unit. Her experience and understanding of the publishing and creative process spans the multiple platforms that now constitute media. This positions her uniquely to address the Group's integrated foray onto the new media landscape. She is a graduate from Oxford University in Politics, Philosophy & Economics..



Rahul Kanwal
Executive Editor, Headlines Today
Keynote Session: Going For Broke
Rahul Kanwal is the Executive Editor of India's premier English news channel Headlines Today. As the fearless voice of young India, the channel has broken a series of path-breaking investigative exposes over the past few months.

Rahul anchors the channel's flagship news show Centre Stage, at 9:00 p.m. on weeknights. The show stands out for its no holds barred discussions and well packaged stories. The country's most powerful ministers, politicians, businessmen and film stars are regular guests on the show.

Rahul is the youngest Editor of a news channel in the country and is credited with establishing Headlines Today as a force to reckon with in the Indian news space. The channel is constantly experimenting and coming up with innovative new formats of presenting news, strongly differentiating itself from other news channels.

 


Koel Purie Rinchet
Actor & TV Anchor
Session Careers: Believe in Yourself; Session Cinema: No Grit, No Glamour

Koel Purie Rinchet is an actor and a talk show host. She made her debut with the critically acclaimed Everybody Says I'm Fine. Ms. Rinchet has acted in over 20 films in India, UK and Canada. She was recently seen in Rock On!! and Amal. She hosts the popular talk show, On the Couch With Koel for the news channel Headlines Today.

 

 

 

Prabhu Chawla
Editorial Director, India Today Group
Concluding Session - Politics: The Good, The Bad & The Ugly

Prabhu Chawla, Editorial Director of India Today Group of publications, is among India’s influential political commentators in print and television. His historical perspective and unmatched record in exposing the transgressions of successive governments have won him many honours including the Padma Bhushan. Under his editorial leadership, India Today has consolidated its preeminent position in authoritative and analytical journalism. Chawla hosts an award winning weekly chat show Seedhi Baat on Aaj Tak, India’s popular news channel. Formerly, he was the editor of the Indian Express and the Financial Express. He is working currently on his memoirs, a chronicle of his close encounters with twelve prime ministers and other political, and corporate leaders, who shaped the political and economic history of modern India.
FROM THE CHAIRMAN
Aroon Purie
Chairman &
Editor-in-Chief
The India Today Group
"So much is said about the young nation that India is ---- about the advantage this gives us over the rest of the world which has largely ageing populations. But our young men & women could turn around and ask: “What are you doing for us? To expose us to new ideas, to charge us with the self confidence we need to face life, to show us the road to maximum achievement?”

At India Today we decided to put our effort where our heart is and so we bring you our latest venture: the India Today Youth Summit, which we hope will become an annual date to remember. The aim of this daylong event is to give young people the unique opportunity to interface with their Icons --- Achievers who have struggled, overcome and today are acknowledged leaders in their fields. Come, listen to them and be inspired by them as they recount their unique experiences and give you their Mantras of Success."
