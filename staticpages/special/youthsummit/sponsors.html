JAYPEE GROUP

Jaypee Group, an infrastructure conglomerate with a turnover of over
Rs. 10,000 crore, has formidable presence in Engineering & Construction, Power,
Cement, Real Estate, Hospitality, Expressways & Education ( not-for-profit) .

The engineering and construction wing of the Group is an acknowledged leader in the construction of multi-purpose river valley and hydropower projects and has participated in projects that have added over 8840 MW of Hydroelectricity to the National grid between 2002 to 2009.

Jaypee Group is the 3rd largest cement producer in the country with an aggregate installed capacity of 22.80 MnTPA* and is slated to be a 37.55 MnTPA by FY12 (expected) with Captive Thermal Power plants totaling 702 MW. (*includes 2.2 MnTPA capacity in Joint Venture with SAIL)

The Group with its operational power plants - 300 MW Baspa-II (Himachal Pradesh) and 400 MW Vishnuprayag (Uttarakhand) is India’s largest Private sector Hydropower producer and is on its way to be an integrated power producer with expansion in Thermal, Wind & Power Transmission. By 2012, the total hydropower capacity will be 1700 MW with the commissioning of 1,000 MW Karcham-Wangtoo project while the total power generation (including Thermal) for the Group shall be 2200 MW. The Group is poised to be a 13,470 MW power producer by 2019.

The Group is a pioneer in the development of India’s first golf centric Real Estate. Jaypee Greens - a world class fully integrated complex at Greater Noida consists of an 18 hole Greg Norman Golf Course. Stretching over 452 acres, it also includes residences, commercial spaces, corporate park, entertainment and nature in abundance. Jaypee Greens also launched its second project in Noida in November 2007. India’s First Wish Town, is an Integrated Township spread over 1162 acres of land comprising one 18 hole and two 9 hole golf facility & world class residences.

The Group is currently implementing 165 km, Noida to Agra, 6/8 lane Yamuna Expressway Project and ribbon development on 6175 acres at five locations along the expressway for commercial, industrial, institutional, residential and amusement purposes, will also be undertaken as an integral part of the project.

The Group owns and operates 4 Five Star Hotels, two in New Delhi and one each in Agra and Mussoorie with a total capacity of 644 rooms. Another state-of-the-art resort and SPA is being set up in collaboration with SIX SENSES at Greater Noida.

The Group supports various educational initiatives at all levels of the learning curve through Jaiprakash Sewa Sansthan, a not-for-profit trust. The Group currently provides education across all spectrum of the learning curve through 19 schools, 3 ITI’s, 2 colleges and 3 universities catering to over 20,000 students.

HILTON

Hilton New Delhi/Janakpuri marks the arrival of the first and only internationally branded full service, upscale property in West Delhi.

The hotel is located approximately 30 minutes' drive from central Delhi, and is strategically close to the International and Domestic airports. At the hotel's doorstep is the Janakpuri West Metro station, providing easy access to the central business district including Connaught Place and Gurgaon. West Delhi is home to an affluent residential part of the city, as well as a significant small to medium enterprise business community.

The ten storey Hilton New Delhi/Janakpuri comprises 228 rooms including 11 suites. The hotel's 65 Executive and Executive Plus rooms start from 33 sq m and are among the city's largest.

For business travelers looking for an enhanced level of service and convenience, the Executive Floor Lounge enables a quick and private check-in/-out service, breakfast, complimentary refreshments served throughout the day, as well as a range of meeting rooms and high speed Internet access to facilitate business needs.

The hotel also has a 990 sqm Grand Ballroom, positioning it among the largest event and conference facilities in New Delhi. Equipped with the latest technology and Hilton's extensive expertise in meetings, the Hilton New Delhi / Janakpuri will provide an exceptional venue for events, social occasions and glamorous weddings.

The hotel's unique food and beverage offerings and wedding banquet capabilities aim to entice the local community and guests with an array of new dining and entertainment options within the neighborhood.

Situated off the hotel's spectacular lobby, Twenty-9 is the all day dining restaurant with an open, interactive kitchen serving Mediterranean, Asian and local favourites. Guests can also indulge in the art of drinking tea at By The Way, a tea house that offers a fine selection of teas and coffees from the tea estates of India and Sri Lanka. For whisky-lovers, a destination bar called Malt & Co. blends contemporary design with a wide choice of Malt whiskies from all over the world.

Signature restaurants planned to open in the next few months are set to revolutionalise the local dining scene. Concepts will include a Chinese Sichuan and Cantonese restaurant, an Italian trattoria and a Mughlai and Kashmiri fine dining restaurant, all created with stylish interiors and an authentic ambience. A bespoke patisserie will offer intricately designed chocolates and desserts, created by one of the country's most renowned pastry chef.
