India Today State of States 2017, Bhubaneswar

About state of the state

With a firm belief that the future of India lies in its 29 states, ever since 2003, India Today’s annual State of the States (SoS) survey has emerged as a benchmark for analyzing the performance of the provinces – the primary units of governance “State of State” is a step ahead of the SoS survey where an X-ray view of each state is prepared, a micro-analysis at the district, city, town and village level would is done which will point out at both the strengths & constructive criticisms
