Pinky: The World's Most Beautiful Vodka

The unique botanical ingredients of Pinky means that there's nothing remotely like it on the shelf. The twelve botanicals include violets, roses, strawberries, and Italian citrus that give Pinky its unique flavor are separately and individually distilled.

This time-consuming process extracts and preserves the delicate aromas from the botanicals, so they can be blended into Pinky Vodka.
