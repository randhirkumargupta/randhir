Writing Instrument Partner
Luxor — Writing Instruments Pvt. Ltd.

Luxor - Writing Instruments Pvt. Ltd. a "Superbrand" was founded by Mr. D.K. Jain in 1963. Luxor has scripted a saga of success over the past five decades and made its mark in both domestic and international markets. Luxor reaches you through more than 100,000 retail outlets across the country and Luxor products are exported to more than 85 countries. Luxor has retained its position as India's top Manufacturer-Exporter of Writing Instruments for the past eight years.

The world values the magic of Brand Luxor - the ingenuity & innovation, care & efforts, which help craft every precision tool. And the Group has brought brands like Pilot, Parker and Waterman to win the hearts of discerning Indians. Luxor is the image leader in the writing instruments market in India, with an extensive range of products catering to specific and varied consumer needs.

Luxor Group has also forayed into stationery and gifting stores under the new retail brands, Luxor Signature and Luxor Express. Luxor Signature is an experiential store for all premium stationery and writing instruments of international acclaim, whereas Luxor Express is a chain of Shop - In- Shop kiosks in outlets like Reliance Timeout offering the best of Luxor in quick time.

For Luxor, consumer loyalty remains the most valuable asset to cherish. And its perpetual commitment to Innovation, Quality and Values re-enforces the rapid growth of this Super Brand across new and diverse ventures - taking the Group ahead towards its vision.
