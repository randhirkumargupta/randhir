Banasthali Vidyapith
 

Banasthali Vidyapith is the largest fully residential women's university in the world, doing pioneering work in its field for more than eight decades!

Banasthali originated in only of its kind circumstances when a father lost his promising daughter and decided to train others the same way. The concept of education for a girl child virtually didn't exist at that time. The founders went door to door to recruit students. There was no question for charging any fee for the initial few decades.

With that humble beginning, today it stands as one of the world's largest fully residential women's university having more than 11000 students on its 850-acre campus situated amidst rural setting in Rajasthan and having a distinct educational ideology and offering a variety of programmes from nursery up to doctoral level across a wide spectrum of disciplines to prepare enlightened citizens with strong value-base.

Vidyapith's alumni have became governors, ministers, parliament/assembly speakers, leading business personalities, noted academic, international athletes and distinguished national artists and musicians.
