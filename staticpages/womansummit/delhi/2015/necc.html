Associate Sponsor
Venkateshwara Hatcheries Group
 
Year of Establishment : 1971 Activities include

  • Indigenous Genetic Research and Development of high yielding layer and broiler breeding stock, acclimatized to Indian agro-climatic and management conditions. This has enabled Indian poultry industry to achieve best productivity levels - comparable to the best in the world.

  • Production of a wide range of poultry vaccines produced from SPF eggs, providing a dependable health cover.

  • Production of SPF eggs (one of the four or five units in the world : only one of its kind in the developing world).

  • Production of farm and hatchery automation equipment and environment control systems.

  • Value addition through poultry processing, further Processing and egg processing.

  • Pet Care Products.

  • Nutritional Health Products.

  • Poultry Diagnostic & Research Centre, Regional / Satellite laboratories, and nationwide of over 100 diagnostic & research laboratories and technical service centers, providing round-the-clock technical support, free of cost to the farmers.

  • Dr. B. V. Rao IPMT - of a unique institute for imparting theoretical and practical, hands - on - the job training to entrepreneurs in poultry industry. So far trained more than 4000 persons, including several candidates from other developing countries - and those sponsored by FAI.

  • Unification and empowerment of farmers, by establishing National Egg Coordination Committee (NECC) and Bromark thereby ensuring a remunerative price for eggs.

Number of employees: Over 10,000
Group Turnover : 3,000 Crores.
