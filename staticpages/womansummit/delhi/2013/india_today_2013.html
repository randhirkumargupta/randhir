INDIA TODAY GROUP DIGITAL (ITGD) is the digital arm of the INDIA TODAY GROUP. ITGD is an umbrella for the contents from the entire India Today Group. ITGD repurposes content from its flagship magazine, India Today; leading news channels - Aaj Tak, Headlines Today, business magazine, Business Today, and Indian editions of the international power brands like Cosmopolitan, Harper's Bazaar, Good Housekeeping, Travel Plus and Men's Health. The objective of the division is to ensure that the premium content of the India Today Group is available in several digital formats whether it is the Internet, SMS (52424), Voice (5052424) or digital subscriptions. Syndications Today, also a part of ITGD, is responsible for syndicating news, articles, images of the group. IndiaTodayImages.com - is a portal which syndicates over 7,50,000 images owned by the India Today Group.

ITGD is the biggest and the fastest-growing online group in India under News and Information category, as per comScore July 2012 report. With the speed at which the group is rising, the Group has surpassed some of the strongest media companies in India. A team of in-house developers, designers, editorial and marketing professionals work round the clock to maintain and promote the India Today Group digital properties. Collectively the online group reaches over 80 million users across the globe.

India Today Group websites maintained by ITGD, comprise of diversified portals like indiatoday.in, aajtak.in, businesstoday.in, wonderwoman.in, menshealth.in, cosmopolitan.in and learntoday.in which have rich content to cater people of different likes and interests. The portals cover news, sports, lifestyle, love and relationship, e-commerce and many more genres through varied mediums like text, audio, videos, images and live television. All the top brands have their digital applications developed by ITGD for leading platforms - Android, iOS, Symbian, Blackberry, SmartTVs and many more. ITGD is also responsible for the maintenance and promotion of 14 digital magazines, which can be read on tablets, smart phones and PCs.
