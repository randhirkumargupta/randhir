HYATT REGENCY
 

Hyatt Regency Delhi, one of largest 5-star hotels in New Delhi, India, is located in the commercial hub of Bhikaji Cama Place, within a few-minutes' drive from the Indira Gandhi International Airport and the city's domestic airport. The hotels close proximity to the embassies, consulates and corporate headquarters, makes it one of the most preferred business hotels in the city.

The 507 rooms and suites at Hyatt Regency Delhi are equipped with modern amenities and high-speed Internet access to support guests' business needs, while all seven floors offer panoramic views of the city and landscaped gardens. The hotels newly designed Regency Club Lounge is the perfect place to conduct brief business meets, entertain guests or simply unwind at leisure after a busy day. The 5-star hotel is also a benchmark of excellence for fine dining in New Delhi and offers an exceptional range of award-winning restaurants and bars.

Guests can enjoy numerous facilities for leisure, including an outdoor swimming pool and the Club Olympus Fitness Centre & Spa, or experience the best in shopping at the hotel's multilevel retail complex. Our prime location is ideal for guests who wish to explore the main attractions in the city or visit various theme parks, beautiful golf courses and recreational sites, as all the details of these excursions can be arranged by the Concierge service.

The proximity of Hyatt Regency Delhi to numerous business centers and outstanding conference facilities makes it the ideal place to plan business meetings. Hyatt Regency Delhi is the only hotel in the Delhi which offers free internet for all guest. For the past 30 years this 5-star hotel has been a leading wedding venue as well. With the upcoming events space, enhanced outdoor catering services, fully serviced apartments, the hotel is positioning to meet a broad spectrum of clients' business and leisure requirements over the years to come.
