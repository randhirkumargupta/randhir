About

1500 years ago India taught the world to count. It is time for the Indian ingenuity to conquer again! It is time for India to take the lead and transform into a global design and manufacturing hub.The 'Make in India' initiative is positioned to do just that. It is a powerful call to action to domestic and multinational companies to manufacture in India. It is an invitation to potential partners and investors, around the world, to invest in India. We are on the path to self-reliance and enhanced economic growth. Be a part of this revolution! Watch the 'Make in India' television series that celebrates this innovative spirit of India, where different sectors will be discussed and one significant company under each sector will be profiled.

Series airs Sun, 6:30 pm and repeats next Saturday at 3:30 pm, only on India Today Televsion.
