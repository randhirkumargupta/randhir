About ReNew Power

The opening episode of the ‘Make in India’ series will profile the company ReNew Power. ReNew Power Ventures Pvt. Ltd. is an Independent Power Producer (IPP) of clean energy with more than 1,600 MW of commissioned and under-construction clean energy assets. Of this more than 745 MW assets are already commissioned. Currently, it operates in eight states across the country. Additionally, it has a development pipeline of more than 1GW solar, wind and distributed solar projects.

ReNew Power is committed to leading a change in the country’s current energy portfolio by delivering cleaner and smarter energy choices and thereby reducing India’s carbon footprint. ReNew Power’s mission is to play a pivotal role in meeting India’s growing energy needs in an efficient, sustainable and socially responsible manner. The company creates value through reliable and efficient generation of non-conventional power through innovation in solar and wind power solutions. ReNew Power is backed by marquee investors such as Goldman Sachs, Abu Dhabi Investment Authority, Asian Development Bank, and Global Environment Fund. ReNew Power has raised total equity of $655 million. The company currently has an asset base in excess of $1.5 billion.

To know more about ReNew Power log on to http://www.renewpower.in/
