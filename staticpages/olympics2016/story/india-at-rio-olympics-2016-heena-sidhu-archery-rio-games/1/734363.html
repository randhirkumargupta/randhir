India so far on day two...

Indian shooters continued to disappoint as Heena Sidhu failed to qualify for the finals of 10m Air Pistol event. At 23:45 IST, India take on Russia in the quarters.

Indian women's hockey team making a return to the Olympic stage after 36 years made an impressive start to their campaign. India staged a comeback from 0-2 deficit to hold Japan to a 2-2 draw. For India, Rani Rampal and Lilima Minz were the goal-scorers.

India @ Olympics: Sunday action

After a mixed outing on day one, Indian athletes would like to come up with a better show on Sunday. Heena Sidhu kicks off the day in women's 10m Air Pistol qualifying at 17:30 IST followed by the finals later in the day.

The major attraction will be the archery team event - comprising of Deepika Kumari, Bombayla Devi and Laxmirani Majhi. The qualification will be at 17:30 IST and finals at 22:30 IST.

Then the action shifts to hockey where the Indian women's team take on Japan at 19:30 IST.

Another top draw and medal hope, gymnast Dipa Karmakar will be in action at 23:00 IST.

And not to forget the trap shooting qualification at 17:30 IST involving Manavjit Sandhu and Kynan Chenai.
