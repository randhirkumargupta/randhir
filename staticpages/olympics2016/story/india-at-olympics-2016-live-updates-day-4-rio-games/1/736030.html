India at Olympics, Highlights: Relive all the action from Day 4

Boxer Vikas Krishan, archer Atanu Das and men's hockey team were the bright spots in India's campaign on Day 4 at the Rio Olympics. Follow the live updates here.

Sattwik Biswal
Sattwik Biswal
Mail to author
August 9, 2016
Last Updated: 04:42 IST
New Delhi

Hello and welcome to the LIVE updates from Day 4 of the Rio Olympics 2016. (RIO OLYMPICS LIVE COVERAGE)

Highlights:

Vikas Krishan's win brings an end to all the Indian events on Day 4. Join us tomorrow for more from the Rio Games. 

3.09 IST: Boxing, Men's 75kg Middleweight: India's Vikas Krishan beats USA's Charles Conwell in preliminary bout to begin his campaign on a high note. Vikas won all the three rounds on points to enter the pre-quarterfinals.

3.03 IST: Boxing: Vikas was pushed on the back-foot on a few occasions by Conwell in the initial stages of the second round but the Indian puglist came back well in the end. Vikas leads by 4 points now.

2.59 IST: Boxing: An action-packed first round comes to an end with both boxers landing punches on each other. Vikas leads Conwell by 3 points.

2.56 IST: Boxing: Focus now shifts to boxer Vikas Krishan where he faces Charles Conwell of USA in the men's 75kg event.

1.05 IST: Meanwhile, Jitu Rai, who missed out on a medal in the 10m Air Pistol event will take part in 50m Air Pistol today at 17.30 IST. Jitu, who is ranked No.2 in the 50m category, is gearing up for the event along with compatriot Prakash Nanjappa. 

Pistol chief coach Smirnov Pavel has said Jitu is ready to shine in Wednesday's event.

"I can only talk about Jitu and he's ready. To me Jitu Rai is a diamond." 

1.00 IST: BOXING - We're less than two hours away from India's first boxing match in Rio. Vikas Krishnan takes on Albert Shone in the men's middleweight (75kg) preliminary bout at 2.45 IST. The winner will reach the quarter-finals.

23:30 IST Sania Mirza-Rohan Bopanna seeded fourth in Rio 2016. To start mixed doubles campaign against Stosur-Peers on Thursday.

India on Day 4 so far...

India had a mixed day with archer Atanu Das and men's hockey team keeping the hopes alive. While, shooter Heena Sidhu  and rower Dattu Bhokanal disappointed.

Dattu Bhokanal kicked off the day by finishing fourth in the individual sculls quarterfinal as Heena having another shot for redemption failed to qualify for the finals in the 25m Pistol event.

However, later in the evening, Atanu Das and the men's hockey team gave some reasons to cheer. The Indian archer was in brilliant form to get through his two elimination rounds and reach the pre-quarters. In hockey, India played spiritedly and survived a scare to beat Argentina 2-1. 

22:46 IST  There is only one event left for the day for India. Vikas Krishan will be in action in middleweight (75 Kg) boxing at 2:45 AM IST.

India @ Olympics: Tuesday action

After a disappointing Day 3 at the Rio Olympics, the onus is again on the athletes to raise their game as India's quest for the first medal continues. All eyes would be on rower Dattu Bhokanal, who kicks off the day for India at Rio in the men's singles sculls quarterfinal.

Then the action will move to archery and shooting where Heena Sidhu will be eyeing redemption in women's 25m Air Pistol qualification. While archer Atanu Das will be seen in action in men's individual round of 64.

The men's hockey team will be again in action today after a heartbreaking 1-2 loss to Germany yesterday in the dying last seconds. India clash against Argentina in a pool B match in the evening.
