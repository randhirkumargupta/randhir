Medal winners at London Olympics

Vijay Kumar won the first silver medal in the London Olympics for India, bagging the metal in 25m rapid fire pistol shooting event.
Yogeshwar Dutt has been one of the biggest surprises at London Olympics, clinching bronze in men's  50kg freestyle wrestling.
Sushil Kumar brought home a silver medal from London Olympics in men's 66kg freestyle wrestling.
Saina Nehwal won the bronze medal in badminton, though she did not quite get the win she may have wanted, as her opponent had to forfeit the match.
Mary Kom has been a surprise package. She won the bronze in flyweight category of women's boxing. The event made its debut in the 2012 Games.
Gagan Narang bagged the first medal for India at the London Olympics, winning bronze in 10m air rifle event.
