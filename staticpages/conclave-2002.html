FLASHBACK 2002: OPPORTUNITIES AND THREATS
January 20-22, 2002, Taj Palace Hotel, New Delhi
INDIA TODAY, the largest and the most influential weekly newsmagazine in South Asia, hosted the first INDIA TODAY CONCLAVE on 20-22 January 2002.
The theme of the Conclave was ‘India Tomorrow: Opportunities and Threats’. It focused on the geopolitical and strategic implications of the turmoil for South Asia in the wake of the global war on terrorism.
It explored the implications on governance, security and the economy of the region. Leaders of government and business, analysts and representatives of think tanks from India and around the world were present at this select gathering, offering interaction at the highest levels of decision-making.
