FLASHBACK 2005: PERCEPTION VERSUS REALITY
February 25, 2005, Taj Palace Hotel, New Delhi
After three successful Conclaves, India Today hosted ‘India Tomorrow 2005: Perception vs. Reality’ on the 25th and 26th February of 2005. The aim of the Conclave was to liberate the India trapped between perception and reality, something India Today as a news magazine has been doing for decades. The distinguished delegates and guests were engaged in talks updating the perceptions about India, adding new ones and debunking a few, thereby making reality more comprehensible. Conclave 2005 became a powerhouse of ideas, which could change the stereotype images India is clouded with and redeem reality.
