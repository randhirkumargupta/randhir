Over the years the name of Vidyamandir Classes (VMC) has become synonymous with success in IIT-JEE. Vidyamandir has over the years designed, delivered, perfected and innovated the art and science of teaching and guiding students for entrance exam for the IITs, which are recognized as the Premier Technology Institutions Across the world.

In 1990, VMC was the first IIT-JEE coaching institute to introduce an admission and talent screening test prospective students.

Our goal at Vidyamandir Classes (VMC) is to provide knowledge and guidance and thereby create an environment that not only guides students to the path of success, but also inspires them to recognize and explore their own potential. Vidyamandir Classes (VMC) is run by a group of Ex-IITians who have over the years guided thousands of IIT aspirants to fulfill their dream of getting into the prestigious IITs.

Vidyamandir Classes offers  2 years (class XI & XII), 3 years (class X, XI & XII) and 4 years (class XI, X, XI & XII) classroom programs. The fact that more than 22,000 students appeared for The VMC Test for the limited seats on offer in 2013, is ample testimony to VMC’s popularity and credence within the IIT-JEE aspirant’s community.

In 2011 VMC introduces the School Integrated Program which helps students achieve probably the best in everything that a student can aspire for. It prepares a student with in a school schedule ensuring top success in IIT-JEE, other Engineering Entrance Exams, International Olympiads and excellence in Boards.
