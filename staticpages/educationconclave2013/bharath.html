Bharath University began its journey in the year 1984, as Bharath Institute of Science and Technology, the first self-financing engineering college in the state of Tamilnadu. Established under the aegis of Sri Lakshmi Ammal Educational Trust with the aim of imparting quality education in the field of Science and Technology to the young minds,the institution was accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council, (NAAC) and the courses offered were also accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi.

As recognition of the quality of education and its service to the meritorious students the institution was awarded the University status in the year 2003 by University Grants Commission (UGC) under section 3 of the UGC act 1956.

We are a multi campus university having campuses at Velacherry, Selaiyur, Chromept in Chennai and also a campus in Puducherry with a combined area of more than 100 acres. Presently the university offers more than 35 undergraduate programmes, 80 post graduate programmes and Ph.D research programmes in the fields of engineering, science, dental and medical sciences. In addition to the conventional courses, it also offers highly specialized courses in emerging and innovative fields and adopts the Choice Based Credit System.

The university has tie ups with more than 25 foreign institutions enabling student exchange, faculty exchange, semester abroad programmes, joint research  projects etc., The Training and Placement cell in collaboration with corporate giants like IBM, Nokia, SAP etc., trains the students in  communication skills, industry relevant, job oriented short term programmes making them more employable.

Inline with its goal of kindling the research interest in the students, the university has established research centres in each of the faculties with modern equipments and facilities that encourage the students to take up research on various projects funded internally and also by various Government agencies. Consultancy projects are also taken up by the faculty and the students are given hands on training on real time projects.

Sports facilities on par with international standards encourage the students to hone their sporting skills. The university provides all the necessary support including free boarding and lodging, special diet, sponsorship for participating in state, national and international level sporting events and financial assistance to deserving students admitted under the sports quota. A variety of extracurricular activities like involvement in National Cadet Corps (NCC), National Service Scheme (NSS), Youth Red Cross (YRC), Personality development programmes, Yoga and meditation classes, Literary clubs, Fine arts clubs, Inter college cultural events provide an opportunity to students to develop as multi skilled technocrats with a social awareness and responsibility.

The university is strategically located and is well connected to all parts of the city by rail and road. Transport facilities at subsidized rates are provided to day scholars. Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with a wide variety of menu ranging from north Indian, south Indian to western and continental, emergency medical facilities, round the clock security and a homely ambience ensure that our hostel students never feel away from home.  Scholarships and Free ships are offered to meritorious, deserving students and also to students from economically and socially backward students.

With its state of the art infrastructure, modern laboratories, highly qualified faculty, internationally acclaimed curriculum, excellent research facilities backed by 30 years of legacy in the field of technical education, Bharath University continues to be the most sought after education destination.
