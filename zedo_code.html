Lately, Parineeti Chopra has been in the news for more reasons than one. For starters, the actress is still riding high on her Golmaal Again success, which crossed Rs 200 crore at the box-office. She has also just been roped in for Kesari, opposite Akshay Kumar.

There's excitement in her professional, as well as her personal life. It is a known fact that Parineeti was in a long-term relationship with filmmaker Maneesh Sharma. However, the two reportedly called it quits last year. After this, there were rumours that Parineeti was seeing an assistant director.

Now, it seems that Maneesh and Parineeti have rekindled their romance. According to a report in Mumbai Mirror, Parineeti is back with her ex-boyfriend. The couple was even spotted at a private party recently, and have been spending a lot of time together lately.

So the question is, has Parineeti really moved ahead with Maneesh? Only time will tell.
